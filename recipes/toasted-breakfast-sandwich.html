Toasted Breakfast Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Sausages x2
  • Rashers x2
  • Black Pudding
  • White pudding
  • Egg x1
  • 2 slices of Bread of your choice
  1. prepare your ingredients, take your black and white pudding and cut off 2-3 pieces of each.
  2. prepare a pan with your choice of lubricant, oil, low cal spray
  3. place the pan on a cooker at gasmark 4 place ingredients into pan
  4. for sausages, once placed in pan, pierce with a sharp knife for each sausage
  5. make sure to rotate ingredients with relative frequency to avoid them burning/sticking to the pan.
  6. For the egg, prepare a small pan and heat with gas mark 4
  7. When pan is sufficiently hot, crack egg shell on side of pan and release contents into pan, avoid getting egg shell fragments
  8. flip routinely to avoid burning the egg, once the egg is sufficiently
  9. Place 2 slices of bread into toaster
  10. Toast for 90 seconds or until the bread is sufficiently brown
  11. remove toast from toaster and apply butter to toasted surface
  12. place ingredients onto the toast, cover with the 2nd piece of toast.
  13. halve the sandwich
  14. serve and enjoy
