Egg Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 4 spring onions
  • cup full of basmati rice

Steps

  1. break 3 eggs into cup and stir till properly mixed
  2. add rice and boiling water to pot, place on gas mark 4 for 15mins
  3. dice 4 spring onions into fine pieces.
  4. once rice is boiled, place in collander and allow water to drain
  5. add oil to pan and turn cooker to gas mark 4
  6. add spring onions and rice to pan, then pour egg mixture over the rice
  7. use spatula to evenly spread mixture through the rice.
  8. mix continuosly with spatula for 5-10mins to allow for even cooking.
  9. take off of heat, split into 4 portions, enjoy.
