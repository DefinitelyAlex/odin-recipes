Cajun Rice

This is an example image for the dish cajun rice.

  • rice
  • 3 half peppers
  • onions
  • 5% fat mince
  • 4 strips of Unsmoked Bacon
Steps for Cajun Rice
  1. Take 500g of 5% fat mince and place into pan.
  2. Take onion and finely dice it into pieces, put these pieces in with the mince
  3. Take 4 bacon strips and firmly dice, place in pan with mince and onion
  4. Turn cooker to gas mark 4 place pan onto active hob.
  5. Once mince has browned thoroughly, add two teaspoons of Cajun Seasoning to mix, along with a dash of Worchestershire Sauce
  6. Dice 3 half peppers 1 red, 1 green, 1 yellow, and add to the mixture
  7. Take 1 tin of Kidney beans, 1 tin of Mixed Beans and 1 tin of Black beans, place these into a collander over a sink
  8. Wash the beans with water before allowing the water to drain, add the beans to the pan.
  9. Boil kettle with Water
  10. Take oxo cube, break the cube up and place it into a measuring jar.
  11. pour 150-200ml of boiling water into the measuring jar, stir until no bits of the oxo cube remain.
  12. Along side step 4, take Chicken stock cube, and break into pieces, place in pot
  13. add boiling water and cupfull of rice to the pot, place on active hob
  14. once rice has cooked, place into collander and allow to drain
  15. From step 11, take the measuring jug and pour contents into pan
  16. add rice to pan and stir mixture
  17. keep stirring for 5-10mins
  18. Seperate into 4 portions
